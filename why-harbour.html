The approach

Why Harbour

Structured back-office support with defined scope — for organisations that need reliability, not an open-ended assistant arrangement.

Focused administrative delivery. Tenancy documentation, charity governance, invoicing, and records management are handled within an agreed scope, allowing you to concentrate on properties, beneficiaries, and organisational priorities.

Transparent reporting. Written email summaries confirm completed work and outstanding decisions. Scope is agreed at the outset; additional tasks are not undertaken without prior approval.

Dedicated practice capacity. Retainer places are limited to ensure accuracy, consistency, and timely delivery. Based in Plymouth, serving clients remotely throughout the United Kingdom.

Operated by Alexandra Ionescu · MSc Management · property and charity administration

  • Micro-charities and community organisations
  • Training providers maintaining trainee records

Outside scope
