Harbour Admin

Meet Alexandra.

Independent back-office practice — operational reliability for organisations that need structured administration without employing another member of staff.

After years supporting landlords, charities, and professional organisations, I created Harbour Admin to provide dependable back-office operations with agreed scope and documented progress — so you can focus on properties, beneficiaries, learners, or client work.

My background is in property administration, charity governance support, and records management. I hold an MSc in Management from the University of Plymouth and have worked across property, training, and charity sectors since 2016.

Harbour Admin is based in Plymouth and serves clients remotely throughout the United Kingdom. Romanian–English business document support is available as an additional service.

Experience

  • Property administration coordinator — lettings and portfolio records (2016–2019)
  • Self-employed administrative support — property, training, and charity clients (2020–2024)
  • MSc Management, University of Plymouth (2019)
  • WordPress content maintenance and records remediation projects

Who I work with

  • Small landlords (approximately 5–25 properties)
  • Micro-charities and community organisations
  • Training providers maintaining trainee or course records
  • Small professional practices with invoicing and records needs
  • Organisations with spreadsheet or CRM data requiring review

How I work

Defined scope, transparent reporting, and limited client capacity — so your administration stays accurate and on time.
