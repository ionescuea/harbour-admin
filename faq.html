Common questions

FAQ

Practical answers about how Harbour Admin works. If your question is not covered here, please email enquiries@harbouradmin.co.uk.

Do I need a monthly contract?
Not necessarily. You can start with a fixed-price project such as a data health check, or choose a monthly retainer if ongoing support suits you.
Can you work remotely?
Yes. Harbour Admin is based in Plymouth and works with clients throughout the UK by email, shared folders, and agreed online tools.
How do retainers work?
You choose a package with a defined hourly allowance. Work stays within agreed scope, with weekly email summaries and approval before anything outside scope. See Packages for details.
Can you sign an NDA?
Yes. Confidentiality is standard for work involving tenant, beneficiary, trainee, or client records.
How do you communicate?
Primarily by email, with a weekly written summary. Brief calls can be arranged for scoping or complex questions during UK business hours.
How quickly can work begin?
After an initial enquiry and agreed scope, most retainers and fixed-price projects can start within one to two weeks. Urgent records work may be possible sooner — please say if you have a deadline.
Can you work with our existing Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace?
Yes. Harbour Admin works within your existing tools — shared mailboxes, OneDrive or Google Drive, Excel or Google Sheets, and your CRM — using minimum access only.
Who is Harbour Admin suited to?
Small landlords, micro-charities, training providers, and small professional practices with defined administrative needs. See About for the full list.
What is outside scope?
Legal, tax, or payroll advice; open-ended personal assistant cover; telephone sales; out-of-hours support; and complex web or software development. Full details are on Why Harbour.
